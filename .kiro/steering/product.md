# Product Overview

This document provides a high-level overview of the Subscription Tracker application and its purpose.

## Purpose

The Subscription Tracker is a full-stack dashboard application designed to help individuals and organizations track and manage their subscriptions efficiently. It provides a comprehensive dashboard interface with visual analytics and management tools to give users better visibility and control over their recurring expenses.

## Key Features

- **Subscription Management**: Add, edit, delete, and categorize subscriptions
- **Financial Dashboard**: Track MRR/YRR with analytics and expense forecasting
- **Billing Tools**: Upload and link invoices to subscriptions
- **Alerts & Reminders**: Get notified before renewals via email and integrations
- **Renewal Calendar**: Visualize upcoming renewals in a calendar view
- **Team Collaboration**: Share subscription management with team members
- **Import/Export**: Transfer data via CSV import/export

## Target Users

The application targets three main user segments:
- **Individuals** (Basic Plan): People who want to track personal subscriptions
- **Professionals** (Pro Plan): Power users who need advanced analytics and features
- **Organizations** (Team Plan): Teams that need collaborative subscription management

## Business Value

- **Cost Visibility**: Provides clear insights into recurring expenses
- **Renewal Control**: Prevents unexpected charges through timely reminders
- **Expense Optimization**: Helps identify duplicate or unnecessary subscriptions
- **Time Savings**: Centralizes subscription management in one platform
- **Team Coordination**: Enables organizations to collaboratively manage subscriptions
